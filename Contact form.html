ABOUT US

Board Game Share was an inspirational idea that occurred to one board young woman in need of a board game, and it spiralled on from there.

With the help of her enthusiastic friends with a basic understanding of coding the Board Game Share was born.

We aim to bring board games off the dusty shelves and back to the people in need of some fun.

We’re building a community of sharers and gamers linking people together to be able to enjoy the fun that’s to be had with board games without the expense of investing in a full set.

Explore the site, explore the games and never be board again!
